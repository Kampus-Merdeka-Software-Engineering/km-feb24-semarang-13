Superstore Giants has exhibited consistent annual growth across its diverse portfolio of 1,833 products, categorized into three primary groups: Office Supplies, Furniture, and Technology. Despite a general upward trend, 2017 saw a deceleration in growth, with a rate of 15.94%, compared to 30.14% in 2016. To regain growth momentum, a detailed analysis of customer segmentation (consumer, corporate, home office) and geographic distribution (California, Texas, New York) is essential. Understanding customer behavior will enable targeted strategies for continued growth.


Superstore Giants' product portfolio is divided into three main categories: Office Supplies, Furniture, and Technology. The Office Supplies category includes nine sub-categories: Binders, Storage, Paper, Appliances, Art, Supplies, Labels, Envelopes, and Fasteners. The Furniture category comprises four sub-categories: Chairs, Tables, Bookcases, and Furnishings, with chairs representing the top-performing sub-category. The Technology category consists of four sub-categories: Phones, Accessories, Copiers, and Machines. Notably, Furniture accounts for 44.3% of total sales, indicating robust performance. However, fasteners in the Office Supplies category are identified as underperforming. Understanding market trends and customer preferences is crucial for optimizing product offerings to meet customer needs effectively.
