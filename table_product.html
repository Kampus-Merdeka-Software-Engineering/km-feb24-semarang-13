Sales of Products Based On Segmentation


This company has three product categories namely Office Supplies, Furniture, and Technology, among the three categories furniture has a percentage of 44.3% which can be said to be a good performance in overall sales. Of the three categories are divided into sub-categories, in Office Supplies has nine sub-categories namely Binders, Storage, Paper, Appliances, Art, Supplies, Labels, Envelopes, and Fasterners, then in Furniture has four sub-categories namely Chairs, Tables, Bookcases, and Furnishings, finally in the technology category has four sub-categories namely Phones, Accessories, Copiers, and Machines. In the furniture category, chairs are the top sub-category and the underperformers are fasterners in the office supplies category.
